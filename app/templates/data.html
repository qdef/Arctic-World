Real-time statistics about the Arctic

It is no secret that because of global warming, the volume and surface of arctic sea ice are decreasing every year,
leading to an increase of the sea water level that has critical consequences around the globe.
On this page we gather real-time key statistics about arctic floe size, temperatures in various arctic areas
and estimations of the number of wild polar bears around the north pole.
