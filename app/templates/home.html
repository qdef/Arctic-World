Welcome to the website of arctic landscapes and polar bears.




Did you know?
The word "arctic" comes from the greek word "αρκτος", which means "bear".
This is because you can find polar bears only around the north pole,
and by opposition, Antarctica is the "pole without bears".
However, you can find pretty cool penguins down there!
